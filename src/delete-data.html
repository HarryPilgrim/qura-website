Data Retention

When you delete data:

  • Immediate: Data is removed from your account instantly
  • 30 days: Permanently deleted from our servers
  • Backups: Removed from backups within 90 days

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., subscription records for tax purposes).

FAQ

Q: Will deleting data affect my subscription?

No, your subscription remains active even if you delete personal data.

Q: Can I recover deleted data?

No, deletion is permanent. Export your data first if you want to keep a copy.

Q: How long does deletion take?

In-app deletions are immediate. Email requests are processed within 7 days.

Q: What's the difference between this and deleting my account?

This page is for deleting specific data while keeping your account. To delete everything and close your account, visit Delete Account.
