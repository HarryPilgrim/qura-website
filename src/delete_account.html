Delete Your Account

Permanent removal of your Qura account and data

Request Account Deletion

We understand that you may want to delete your Qura account and all associated data. We respect your right to be forgotten and will process your request promptly.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to:

hello@quraapp.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Your registered email address (the one you use to log into Qura)
  • Confirmation statement: "I request the deletion of my Qura account and all associated data"

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete the following data:

  • Account information - Email, name, password
  • Recovery journey data - Streak counts, goals, milestones
  • Personal content - Journal entries, pledges, notes
  • Chat history - All conversations with Aida (our AI companion)
  • Saved content - Favorite passages and bookmarks
  • Triggers and preferences - Your personal settings and configurations
  • Progress tracking - All historical data and achievements
  • User identifiers - Firebase UID and RevenueCat customer ID

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and security purposes, we may retain limited information:

  • Subscription records - Required by tax law (anonymized after 7 years)
  • Transaction history - Kept by Apple/Google per their policies
  • Anonymized analytics - Aggregated usage data with no personal identifiers
  • Legal compliance data - Information we're required to keep by law

Note: Retained data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: We'll confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours
  • Processing: Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Deletion: All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You'll receive a final email confirming deletion is complete

Important Notes

⚠️ This Action is Permanent

Account deletion cannot be undone. All your data, including:

  • Your recovery progress and streak history
  • All journal entries and personal reflections
  • Saved Bible passages and favorites
  • Chat conversations with Aida

...will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.

Active Subscription?

If you have an active subscription, please note:

  • Cancel first: We recommend canceling your subscription through the App Store or Google Play before requesting account deletion
  • No refunds: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription or entitle you to a refund
  • How to cancel:
    • iOS: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Qura → Cancel Subscription
    • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Qura → Cancel Subscription
